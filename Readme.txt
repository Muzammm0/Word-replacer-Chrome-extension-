

Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions.

Toggle the "Developer mode" switch on the top right-hand corner.

Click on the "Load unpacked" button on the top left-hand corner.

Select the folder you created in step 1.

The extension should now be loaded and ready to use. Navigate to any web page and click on the extension icon in the top right-hand corner of your Chrome window.

The options page for the extension should open. Here, you can specify the words you want to replace and their replacements.

Click the "Add" button to add a new word to replace. The word will be added to the list of words to replace below.

Once you have added all the words you want to replace, click the "Save Changes" button to save your changes.

Navigate to any web page and click on the extension icon again. The extension should replace all instances of the words you specified on the options page with their replacements
